Privacy Policy

Big Daves News — effective date: March 2026

Overview

Big Daves News provides headlines, weather, market data, and optional notifications. We aim to minimize collected data and only use information needed to operate core features.

Information We Use

  • Email address when you opt into daily report delivery.
  • Approximate location only when you allow location access for local weather.
  • Push token when you enable notifications to deliver alerts.

How We Use Information

  • Send requested daily emails.
  • Provide local weather and forecast results.
  • Deliver push notifications you opt into.
  • Maintain reliability and prevent abuse of the service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure and data providers required to run the service (for example hosting, weather, and market providers).

Retention

We retain subscriber and device-token records only as needed for active service operation. You may request deletion by contacting support.

Your Controls

  • Disable location in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings or in-app reminder controls.
  • Request account/data removal via support contact.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@bigdavesnews.com

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
