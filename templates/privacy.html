Privacy Policy

Big Dave's News — effective date: April 2026

Overview

Big Dave's News is a personalized news, sports, and entertainment app. We collect limited information needed to personalize your experience, deliver notifications you request, and improve the service. We do not sell your personal data or use it for third-party advertising.

How We Use This Data

  • Personalize your news, Watch, and Sports feeds.
  • Maintain your watchlist, saved articles, and sports favorites across sessions.
  • Deliver push notifications and daily emails you opt into.
  • Show locally-relevant weather (processed on-device; coordinates are not sent to our servers).
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve features.
  • Prevent abuse and maintain service reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by third-party infrastructure providers required to operate the service, including:

  • Render.com — cloud hosting for our API servers.
  • Apple WeatherKit — weather data (Apple's privacy policy applies).
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — push notification delivery.
  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — poster images and show metadata for the Watch tab.
  • ESPN public data feeds — sports schedules, scores, and broadcast information.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Retention

Preferences and interaction history are retained for as long as your installation identifier is active (i.e., while the app remains installed). Push tokens and email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion. We do not retain precise location data.

Your Controls

  • Location: Disable in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services at any time.
  • Notifications: Disable in iOS Settings → Notifications → Big Dave's News, or via in-app Settings.
  • Email reports: Unsubscribe via the link in any email we send.
  • Data deletion: Email support@bigdavesnews.com to request deletion of all data associated with your device or email address.
  • App removal: Deleting the app removes locally stored preferences and resets your installation identifier. Cached data on our servers can be removed on request.

Children's Privacy

Big Dave's News is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
