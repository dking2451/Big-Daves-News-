Quick Troubleshooting

  • Weather not updating: open app Settings and confirm location access is enabled.
  • Push notifications not arriving: ensure reminders are enabled and app notifications are allowed.
  • Market charts not loading: pull to refresh and verify network connectivity.
  • Headlines appear stale: use refresh and confirm data provider availability.
